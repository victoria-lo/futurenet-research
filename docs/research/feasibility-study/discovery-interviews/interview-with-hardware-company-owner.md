# Interview with Hardware Company Owner

**Interviewee:** Nimantha  
**Background:** 2-time startup owner, founder of Ackcio (hardware product company)  
**Context:** Understanding hardware product development considerations for screen time usage research with kids

---

## Hardware Product Development Approaches

### Custom vs. Off-the-Shelf

- **Custom in-house development** - Build from scratch
- **Standard off-the-shelf casing with customization** - Modify existing products
- **Capital intensive** - Requires significant money upfront

### Key Recommendations

- **Leverage existing solutions as much as possible**
- Consider **Android open source**:
  - OS can be modified to suit needs
  - Flash custom OS to the device

---

## Manufacturing Insights

### Factory Selection & Location

- **Shenzhen** has thousands of manufacturing plants
- **Product availability:**
  - Shenzhen likely already has the product you need ready-made
  - Just need to customize it to your requirements
- **Getting started:**
  - Need referrals to factories/manufacturing plants
  - Language barrier: Need to speak Chinese
  
### Challenges Encountered

- **Quality control issues:**
  - Some factories use refurbished parts
  - Quality was at stake

### Contract Manufacturers

- **Legal protections:**
  - NDA clause required
  - Non-compete agreements
  
- **Subcontracting:**
  - Contract manufacturers may subcontract to others
  - They handle QC (Quality Control)

### Software & Testing Strategy

- **Did not give software to Chinese manufacturers**
  - Trust concerns
  - Resulted in testing being done in Singapore instead
  
- **Current approach:**
  - Now working with Singapore-based factory

---

## Product Validation Framework

### The Mom Test

- **Validation metric:** If people are ready to pay for it, then it is validated
- Focus on actual commitment, not just interest

### Vitamins vs. Painkillers

- **Vitamins:** Good to have
- **Painkillers:** Need to have
- Aim to solve real pain points, not just nice-to-haves

### Funding & Validation

- **Kickstarter** - Recommended platform for validation and initial funding

---

## Strategic Advice for Our Project

### Software-First Approach

- **Recommendation:** Try to work with software instead of delving into hardware space
  
- **Rationale:**
  - Hardware slowed them down significantly
  - Requires certifications (regulatory burden)
  - Get to market faster via software

### Business Setup

- **Accelerator programs** - They went through one (beneficial for guidance and network)
- **Company registration:**
  - Required when funds start coming in
  - Process is pretty quick

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Start with software** if possible - faster to market, fewer regulatory hurdles
2. **Validate with real payment commitments** - use platforms like Kickstarter
3. **Solve painkillers, not vitamins** - focus on critical needs
4. **If hardware is necessary:**
   - Leverage existing solutions and open source where possible
   - Be prepared for capital intensity
   - Carefully vet manufacturing partners (quality, trust, legal protections)
   - Consider local manufacturing for better control despite higher costs
